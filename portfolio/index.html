About Me

Hi, I'm Alejandro! I love my sister. I am a full stack web developer at Future-Corp.

A leading company on making websites more accessible for everyone. As a web developer my task is to make sure that everyone that access my website is able to navagate it at ease and enjoy the content and features provided. I want to deliver the best expirience equally to all users.

My websites follow the accessibility standards such as perceivable content and user interaction; as well as captions for audio content and discription of images. I often try to put myself as a user on my websites in order to tackle efficiently accessibility issues.
